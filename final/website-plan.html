Site Purpose:

A website to search for armor, artifacts and job opportunities.

The idea is to have some sort of store for people to buy different artifacts, armor, quests or jobs as we know them today and a form to apply for those quests.

Scenarios:

Where can I find quests to apply for?

What is the best armor people is using to fight dragons?

Color scheme:

For this project the color scheme includes the following:

Charcoal black, bone white, crimson red, iron gray, ember orange, beige.

Typography:

UnifrakturCook for titles or headings or maybe Cinzel decorative.

Lora, Inter or Merriweather. I will test some combinations here.
